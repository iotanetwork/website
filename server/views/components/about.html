We are IOTA Evangelists

IOTA evangelists are people who build a critical mass of support for the IOTA technology, and subsequently establishes IOTA as a technical standard within the global market.

Content Creating

We promote the adoption of IOTA to individuals, groups and organizations through giving talks, writing articles, creating videos.

IOTA Demo

We conduct demonstrations on IOTA for organisations to promote adoption and learning purposes.

IOTA POCs

We help create proof of concepts using IOTA. We also help out other organisations with their use-cases by consulting them on technical lines.
